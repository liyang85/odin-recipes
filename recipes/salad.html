Description

A vibrant mix of fresh greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumbers, offering a crisp and colorful healthy experience.
新鲜蔬菜、樱桃番茄和黄瓜的生动混合，带来清脆多彩的健康体验。

Tossed in a light vinaigrette, this salad delivers a refreshing crunch and natural flavors with every bite.
拌入清淡的油醋汁，这道沙拉每一口都能带来清爽的口感和自然的风味。

Ingredients

  • Fresh Mixed Greens (新鲜混合生菜)
  • Cherry Tomatoes, halved (切半的樱桃番茄)
  • Cucumber, sliced (切片的黄瓜)
  • Red Onion, thinly sliced (切成薄片的红洋葱)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (特级初榨橄榄油)
  • Lemon Juice or Vinegar (柠檬汁或醋)
  • Salt and Black Pepper (盐和黑胡椒)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vegetables (准备蔬菜)

    Wash and dry the greens, chop the tomatoes and cucumbers into bite-sized pieces.
    洗净并擦干生菜，将番茄和黄瓜切成一口大小的块。

  2. Make the Dressing (制作酱汁)

    In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    在小碗中，将橄榄油、柠檬汁、盐和胡椒搅拌均匀。

  3. Combine and Toss (混合搅拌)

    Place vegetables in a large bowl, pour the dressing over them, and toss gently.
    将蔬菜放入大碗中，倒上酱汁，轻轻搅拌。

  4. Serve Immediately (立即享用)

    Plate the salad fresh to ensure the greens stay crisp and crunchy.
    新鲜装盘，以确保生菜保持清脆爽口。
