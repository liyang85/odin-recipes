Description

A mouth-watering plate featuring thick slices of juicy, medium-rare steak with a perfectly seared crust.
这是一盘令人垂涎的厚切多汁牛排，煎得恰到好处，内里呈半熟粉红色。

Served alongside roasted potatoes, asparagus, and cherry tomatoes, it makes for a complete and elegant dinner.
搭配烤土豆、芦笋和樱桃番茄，这是一顿丰盛而优雅的晚餐。

Ingredients

  • Premium Ribeye Steak (优质肋眼牛排)
  • Sea Salt & Black Pepper (海盐和黑胡椒)
  • Fresh Rosemary & Thyme (新鲜迷迭香和百里香)
  • Garlic Cloves, crushed (压碎的蒜瓣)
  • Unsalted Butter (无盐黄油)
  • Olive Oil (橄榄油)

Steps

  1. Season the Meat (给肉调味)

    Pat the steak dry and season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
    将牛排擦干，并在两面撒上足量的盐和胡椒。

  2. Sear the Steak (煎牛排)

    Heat oil in a pan and sear the steak until a golden-brown crust forms.
    锅中热油，将牛排煎至表面金黄焦脆。

  3. Baste with Butter (黄油淋汁)

    Add butter, garlic, and herbs, then spoon the melted butter over the steak repeatedly.
    加入黄油、大蒜和香草，不断将融化的黄油淋在牛排上。

  4. Rest and Serve (静置装盘)

    Let the steak rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing to lock in the juices.
    切开前让牛排静置 5-10 分钟以锁住肉汁。
